## Long description (full store listing)

**Orange AI – Your All-in-One AI Assistant**

Talk and show—don’t just type. Orange AI is an AI-powered assistant that understands images and voice so you can interact the way you prefer.

**• Images**  
Send photos and images to get answers, identifications, or ideas. Whether it’s a document, object, or scene, Orange AI can help you understand and act on it.

**• Voice input**  
Speak your questions and requests. No typing required—just talk and get responses. Great for hands-free use and accessibility.

**• One assistant for everything**  
One app that works with both images and voice. Choose how you want to interact each time and get things done faster.

Download Orange AI and experience a smarter way to get answers and get things done.
